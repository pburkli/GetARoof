GetARoof facilitates the process of finding a matching accommodation for vacations.
  The user may enter his/her goal like "Find a flat for our skiing holidays in
  Zermatt for 1.1.2027 to 8.1.2027. We are 10 people: The grand parents, they need a
  room for themselfs, then the Bürkli family, the need a room with four beds or two
  rooms with two beds, then there is the Windgate familiy, they need a room with
  three beds. A there is a single person wich needs a room for himself. The flat
  should not be more than 10min walk from the cablecar. We cannot pay more than
  3000CHF for the week". The app will then fetch data from many platforms at once
  like booking.com, homeToGo and more. With the aid of AI the app will check
  avaliabity for the requested period and it checks if the accodation matches the
  requirements. It will present the 5 best results to the user with pictures,
  description and exact location. The user will then make his/her choice and click on
  a "book now" button on the selected result. The app will then autonmously perform
  the booking process up until the payment. The user will then be prompted to
  complete the payment.
